IEEE OES Kerala Chapter is dedicated to fostering innovation in oceanic research, underwater technology, and marine sustainability. Join us in shaping the future of oceanic engineering.

About Us

The IEEE OES Kerala Chapter promotes oceanic engineering in Kerala through collaboration with universities, research institutions, and industry leaders. Our mission is to advance marine technology and sustainable ocean solutions.

Technical Activities

Our key areas of research include:

  • Underwater Robotics Development
  • Marine Sensor Networks & Data Analytics
  • Coastal & Oceanic Environmental Monitoring
  • Renewable Energy Solutions for Marine Applications
